# Deploy to Netlify - Quick Guide

Since this is a simple static site (HTML/CSS/JS), the easiest way to deploy it is using **Netlify Drop**.

## Step 1: Prepare your folder
Ensure your folder `CoockiesWeb` has the `index.html` file right at the top level (not inside another subfolder).

## Step 2: Netlify Drop
1. Go to [app.netlify.com](https://app.netlify.com/drop).
2. If you don't have an account, sign up (it's free).
3. Once logged in, go to the **Sites** tab.
4. Drag and drop your entire `CoockiesWeb` folder from your desktop into the area that says "Drag and drop your site folder here".

## Step 3: Success!
Netlify will upload your files and give you a live URL (e.g., `sad-albattani-12345.netlify.app`). You can change this site name in "Site settings" -> "Change site name".

That's it! Your site is live.
